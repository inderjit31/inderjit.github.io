About me

Hello! I'm Inderjit, a dedicated and enthusiastic individual who finds immense joy in unraveling the stories hidden within data. Armed with a BScIT degree from St. Andrews College, Mumbai, Bandra, I've embarked on a journey to become a proficient data analyst. My insatiable curiosity drives me to explore datasets with a critical eye, seeking patterns and insights that can catalyze informed decision-making.

My skill set is a testament to my commitment to excellence. I wield Excel as a versatile tool for data manipulation and insightful reporting. SQL is my language of choice for uncovering hidden gems within databases. With Power BI and Tableau, I create interactive dashboards that empower stakeholders to interact with data firsthand. And while my journey with Python is in its early stages, I'm excited about the possibilities it offers for advanced data manipulation and automation.

With a penchant for continuous learning, I'm always on the lookout for novel challenges that push me to expand my skill set. As I navigate the dynamic landscape of data analytics, I'm committed to contributing my expertise to meaningful projects that span industries and domains. Let's connect and collaborate to harness the potential of data-driven insights together.

Thank you for taking the time to explore my portfolio.
